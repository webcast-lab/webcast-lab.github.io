Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Welcome to Web Video Cast to TV ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our application.

1. Information Collection

We believe in data minimization. When you use "Web Video Cast to TV", we do not collect personal identifyable information unless you voluntarily provide it to us (e.g., through support emails).

  • Local Network Data: To enable casting functionality, the app requires access to your local network to discover and connect to casting devices (Chromecast, Roku, DLNA, etc.). This data is processed locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app's performance and stability.

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining the core functionality of the app (casting videos).
  • Improving user experience and fixing bugs.
  • responding to your customer service requests.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app may allow you to browse websites. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or practices. We recommend reviewing their policies.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect specific personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
